Teachers & Guides

Our teachers have spent decades practicing and teaching meditation globally, offering experienced support for your retreat journey.

Lead Teacher

Acharya Shanti

With over 25 years of monastic and lay teaching experience, Shanti guides our deep Vipassana retreats, offering clear Dharma talks and compassionate individual advice.

Mindfulness

David Mercer

Specializing in secular mindfulness and stress-reduction programs, David helps beginners establish a solid, daily sitting practice with practical tools.

Yoga & Somatics

Elena Rostova

Elena integrates gentle Yin yoga, breath training, and somatic integration to release trauma, relax the nervous system, and prepare the body for sit periods.

Zen Practice

Michael Hsing

A student of the Rinzai Zen tradition, Michael leads walking meditations, Zen gardening, and community chore practices to bring presence to action.

Compassionate Presence

Our guides hold a warm, gentle presence to support you during your stay. We walk alongside you every day of the retreat, ensuring a safe and supportive mental environment.

Our Three Pillars of Guidance

We combine ancient lineages with modern scientific understanding to offer complete support for mind and body.

Monastic Lineage

Deep roots in traditional Vipassana and Zen meditation practices, bringing centuries of proven wisdom to modern retreats.

Secular Mindfulness

Evidence-based mindfulness techniques that reduce stress, regulate emotions, and improve focus in daily secular life.

Somatic & Yin Yoga

Gentle movement practices designed to release physical tension, cultivate body awareness, and ground your meditation practice.
