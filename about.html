For The Mind

We provide a peaceful environment where you can step away from daily life, establish stillness, and learn deep mindfulness techniques.

Our Serene Setting

Located deep within a protected forest reserve, StillMind center is surrounded by centuries-old pine trees, quiet walking trails, and a crystal-clear mountain stream.

The Main Meditation Hall

Constructed from natural cedar wood with large glass windows overlooking the valley. Features comfortable tatami mats, ergonomic cushions, and perfect acoustics for silence.

Comfortable Lodging

Choose between private forest cabins, garden suites, or shared cottages. All spaces feature minimal Zen decor, comfortable bedding, and natural heating.

Organic Nutrition

Enjoy three healthy, organic, plant-based meals prepared daily by our wellness chefs. All ingredients are sourced from our organic garden or local sustainable farms.

Dedicated to Stillness

StillMind Retreats was founded with the single mission of providing a space for silent contemplation. We believe that in absolute silence, the mind naturally settles and heals itself.

Environmentally Conscious
